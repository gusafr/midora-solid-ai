    Common Pitfalls to Avoid¬∂

    ‚ùå Vanity metrics - Tracking AI usage without business impact
    ‚úÖ Solution: Always tie KPIs to business outcomes (revenue, costs, quality)

    ‚ùå Too many metrics - Tracking 50+ KPIs per agent
    ‚úÖ Solution: Start with 8 universal KPIs + 3-5 agent-specific KPIs

    ‚ùå No governance KPIs - Only tracking business metrics
    ‚úÖ Solution: Equally weight governance (bias, privacy, accountability)

    ‚ùå Annual OKRs - Too slow for AI pace
    ‚úÖ Solution: Quarterly OKRs with monthly check-ins

    ‚ùå Agent-only metrics - Ignoring human-AI collaboration
    ‚úÖ Solution: Augmentation factor measures combined human+AI output
