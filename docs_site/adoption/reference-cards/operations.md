## SOLID.AI Operations Mindset

✅ **Do:**
- Design for observability from day one
- Treat AI agents as teammates with roles
- Build automated responses with human oversight
- Create blameless learning cultures
- Monitor ethical signals alongside technical metrics
- Optimize for resilience, not just efficiency

❌ **Avoid:**
- Black-box AI without visibility
- Alert fatigue from noisy signals
- Heroic firefighting vs. systematic improvement
- Deploying without rollback plans
