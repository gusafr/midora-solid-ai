# RFC Template

Use this template to propose changes to the SOLID.AI framework or your organization's implementation.

For the complete markdown template, see:

**[→ Full Template on GitHub](https://github.com/gusafr/midora-solid-ai/blob/main/ADOPTION/TEMPLATES/rfc-template.md)**

---

## Template Sections

The template includes:

1. **Summary** - Brief overview
2. **Motivation** - Problem, goals, non-goals
3. **Proposal** - Design, examples, SOLID.AI alignment
4. **Trade-Offs & Alternatives** - What we're accepting, what we considered
5. **Implementation** - Phases, dependencies, risks
6. **Metrics & Success** - How we measure success
7. **Ethical & Governance** - Stakeholder impact, compliance
8. **Open Questions** - What needs resolution
9. **Discussion & Feedback** - Review history

---

## Usage Instructions

1. **Copy the template** to your repository
2. **Fill in each section** thoughtfully
3. **Share for review** with stakeholders
4. **Iterate based on feedback**
5. **Track status** (Draft → In Review → Accepted/Rejected)
6. **Update as needed** with discussion outcomes
