# Squad Charter Template

Use this template to define your squad's purpose, scope, and operating model.

For the complete markdown template, see:

**[→ Full Template on GitHub](https://github.com/gusafr/midora-solid-ai/blob/main/ADOPTION/TEMPLATES/squad-charter-template.md)**

---

## Template Sections

The template includes:

1. **Squad Identity** - Name, lead, members
2. **Purpose & Mission** - Why the squad exists, vision
3. **Scope & Boundaries** - What's in/out
4. **Key Results & Metrics** - OKRs and success measures
5. **Operating Model** - Rituals, communication, decisions
6. **AI & Automation** - Agents and data contracts
7. **Working Agreements** - How to collaborate
8. **Continuous Learning** - Skill development
9. **Governance & Ethics** - Responsibilities and oversight
10. **Reflection & Evolution** - Charter review cadence

---

## Usage Instructions

1. **Copy the template** to your repository
2. **Fill in each section** with your squad
3. **Review and get buy-in** from team and stakeholders
4. **Publish** to your team wiki or docs
5. **Review regularly** (quarterly recommended)
