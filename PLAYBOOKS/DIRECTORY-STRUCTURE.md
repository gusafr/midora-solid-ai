**Time investment:** 1-1.5 hours total

**Critical insight:** Skip this and you WILL have incidents

---

### ğŸ”§ **Implementation** (5 playbooks)
**Purpose:** Practical guides for building technical capabilities

**When to read:**
- After governance is established
- Ready to deploy agents, processes, data infrastructure
- Need hands-on technical guidance

**Time investment:** 3-4 hours total

**Recommended sequence:**
1. Agents Guide (deploy first agent)
2. Process Mapping (document workflows)
3. Data Spine Structuring (build foundation)
4. Data Analytics (enable learning)
5. Agile Quality (update processes)

**Time investment:** 2-2.5 hours total

**Critical insight:** Technology is easy, people are hard

---
